Sushi

A nice Japanese dinner you can make at home in 10 minutes!

Ingridients

  • Sushi rice
  • Nori seaweed
  • Sushi vinegar
  • Fish, avocado, cucumber, or any other toping, that you like

Cooking instructions

  1. Cook sushi rice for 15 minutes. When cooled, fluff it with a fork.
  2. Prepare rolling mat. Place nori, than rice on top, than put the desired ingridient on one side of the roll
  3. Gently roll the sushi to form a tight roll
  4. Cut it with a wet knife and enjoy!
